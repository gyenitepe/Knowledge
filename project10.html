Key Takeaways & 2026 Focus

Strategic insights from 2025 performance and recommended focus areas for the upcoming year.

üìà Key Findings

Churn-Heavy Mix

64% of requests were Churn Prevention, with Low ROAS and High RPA being the dominant issues. This reactive pattern suggests opportunity for earlier intervention.

Launch Phase Dominates

45% of requests relate to campaign launches. Combined with 64% of conversion issues in early-stage campaigns, suggests focus on launch playbooks.

Higher Stakes on Growth

Growth Opportunity requests average 2x the daily budget ($4.3K vs $2.1K) with much lower churn risk (1.7 vs 3.7).

European Markets Challenge

European markets (IT/ES/FR/NL/DE) appear in 80% of geo-related issues, often struggling with CPI targets.

2

3

4

Improve RL/GL adoption to 90%+ through training and mandatory fields

5

Develop European market strategies to address persistent CPI/performance challenges
